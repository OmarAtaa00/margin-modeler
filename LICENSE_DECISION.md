# License Decision Record

## Current decision

Margin Modeler will remain proprietary during the alpha phase.

Repository license:

```text
Copyright © 2026 Omar AtaaAllah. All rights reserved.
```

The repository should use `LICENSE.txt`, not the MIT License.

## Why MIT was not selected yet

The MIT License is intentionally permissive. It allows other people and
companies to use, copy, modify, merge, publish, distribute, sublicense, and
sell copies, provided the copyright and license notice are retained.

MIT is a good choice when the owner deliberately wants broad open-source use,
including commercial reuse and proprietary forks.

It is not a good default when the owner wants to preserve exclusive commercial
control while the product direction is still being decided.

## Future options

The owner may later choose to:

- keep the project proprietary;
- open-source all or part of it under MIT;
- use a copyleft license for selected components;
- offer separate community and commercial editions; or
- use dual licensing.

A deliberate license review should happen before any open-source license is
published.

## Important distinction

A software license controls legal permission to use and redistribute code.
Windows Authenticode signing verifies publisher identity and file integrity.

Choosing MIT does not sign the application and does not remove SmartScreen,
Defender, or CrowdStrike warnings.
